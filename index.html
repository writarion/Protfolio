Hi, I’m Writarion

Biography

Name: Sabbir Hosen Akash

Artist Name: Writarion

Date of Birth: January 3, 2002

Place of Birth: Sandwip Upazila, Bangladesh

Height: 5'7" (1.70m)

Profession: Singer-Songwriter, Composer, Lyricist, Writer, Music Producer

Music Career

Sabbir’s journey began with Oparok Band in 2021. He became popular for his albums like Mritto Bahok, featuring hits like “Mritto Bahok,” “Moner Dukkho,” and “Harano Golpo.”

Literary Works

His debut book Whispers of the Heart explores themes of love, loss, and growth—just like his music.

Notable Achievements

  • Lead member of Oparok Band
  • Multiple-time Sandwip Music Program winner
  • Released his first solo in 2022
  • Captivating lyrics in pop and rock

Future Goals

Sabbir is expanding his artistic vision, including the development of advanced AI systems that push the boundaries of human imagination.
